Coach

“Better than a thousand days of diligent studies is one day with a good teacher !” 

							At Techninzaz, we coach and enhance every student’s potential to build their success stories not only in colleges, but also in the corporate workplace. We are here with the combination of academic and core corporate industry experience which will equip every student with all the necessary study and career skills.

Educate

"The object of education is to prepare the young to educate themselves throughout their lives."

							At Techninzaz, we are not restricting education only to the old­school books and study notes. We are here to educate and prepare every students with ‘beyond­the­books’ knowledge.

"If you do one thing every day towards your dreams, you're doing much more than most."

Our Campus to Corporate (C2C) module is to inspire every student to develop skills to succeed and become quality professionals in the corporate industry, after all, an inspiration is the best medicine to become the best from an average.
