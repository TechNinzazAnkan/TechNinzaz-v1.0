Master of Computer Application (MCA)

MCA is a post graduation course which is obtained after a Bachelor’s degree.The course is designed to meet the growing demand for qualified professionals in the field of Information technology. MCA programme focuses on Application development and thus emphasises on the latest programming languages and tools to develop a better application.

After MCA degree a candidate faces the real challenges and is provided with job opportunities in top level IT industry and consultancy firms.
